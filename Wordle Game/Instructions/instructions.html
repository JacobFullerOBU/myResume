Instructions for Wordle Game:

Wordle is a word game in which players must create words from a set of given letters. To play Wordle, you will need to follow these steps:
You will be presented with a set of letters, which you will use to form words.
To make a guess, type all 5 letters, and press the enter button or the enter key on the keyboard.
The colors work as follows:
Grey = Letter is not in the word
Yellow = Letter is in the word, just not in this spot
Green = Letter in in the correct spot in the word.
You will have 6 guesses to form the correct word and win the game!
Good luck!
